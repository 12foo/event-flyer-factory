Bernie 2016 Flyers

Print & share flyers for Bernie events

Better together-- that goes double for grassroots politics. Get active, and invite friends, colleagues and total strangers to one of the many Bernie events near you!

This website can help you get started. Put in your zip code below, and you can have a ready-to-print invitation flyer for events near you in less than a minute.

Choose your events

Tap events to put them on your flyer. Tap them again to put them back. If you want to move events up or down on your flyer, tap the little arrows that will appear on the right.

Select a design and layout for your flyer

The design is the general look of the flyer (colors and images), and the layout is the arrangement of the events on it. Click to select a design below, then click again to select a layout.

Print your flyer

Your flyer should be downloading now! Information about printing goes here.
